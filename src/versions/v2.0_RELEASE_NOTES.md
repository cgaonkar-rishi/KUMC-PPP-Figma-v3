## üåü Major Features

### Payment Request Management System
- **Queue-Based Workflow** - Five-tab approval system (Pending Review, Approved, Rejected, Completed, All Requests)

- **Receipt Management** - Drag-and-drop file upload with validation
- **Mileage Calculator** - Automatic calculation for drive/mileage reimbursements
- **Tax Withholding** - Automated tax calculation and tracking
- **Audit Trail** - Comprehensive workflow history with detailed tracking

- **Complete Study Lifecycle** - Create, manage, and track research studies
- **Participant Verification** - Built-in identity verification workflows
- **Address Verification** - USPS-style address validation and standardization
- **Demographics Management** - Full participant data tracking with DOB and Address columns
- **Enrollment Tracking** - Real-time enrollment status and history

### Administrative Features
- **Funding Management** - Grant and funding source tracking with budget allocation
- **Organizations & Locations** - Hierarchical facility and department management
- **Security & Permissions** - Role-based access control with granular permissions
- **Reports & Analytics** - Custom reporting and data insights
